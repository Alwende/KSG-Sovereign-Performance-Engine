# KSG Sovereign Performance Engine (KSG-SPE-016)

## Strategic Objective
To modernize the Kenya School of Government's performance reporting ecosystem. This engine automates the lifecycle of the **Citizen Service Delivery Charter** and **Annual Work Plan (AWP)**, transitioning from manual paperwork to AI-driven governance.

## Key Features
- **Automated Compliance Math:** Instant calculation of variance against PC targets.
- **Vertex AI Narrative Gen:** Automated "Preventive Corrective Actions" grounded in the KSG Productivity Strategy.
- **Executive Visibility:** Real-time dashboard for the Director General and Senior Directors.
- **Regulatory Alignment:** 100% compliant with standard Kenyan Public Service reporting templates.

- **Phase 1-4:** Complete (Initiation, Digitization, Logic, and Execution).
- **Phase 5:** Visualization and Executive Dashboarding (Current).
