# PROJECT CHARTER: KSG SOVEREIGN PERFORMANCE ENGINE (KSG-SPE-016)

## 1. STRATEGIC MISSION
To automate the Performance Reporting lifecycle for the KSG Center for Customer Service Excellence (CCSE). This MVP transitions the School from manual paper-based reporting to an AI-driven Cloud Governance platform.

## 2. THE PROBLEM STATEMENT
Current reporting against the Citizen Service Delivery Charter and Annual Work Plans is manual, asynchronous, and prone to "Reporting Fatigue." Executives lack real-time visibility into % Compliance and Variance across the School.

## 3. TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE (THE MVP)
- **Ingestion:** Digital entry portal for monthly Performance Contract (PC) and Annual Work Plan (AWP) achievements.
- **Intelligence (Vertex AI):** Automated analysis of variances and generation of "Preventive Corrective Actions" grounded in the KSG Productivity Improvement Strategy.
- **Output:** Automated generation of the "Commitments and Standards Compliance Report."
- **Governance:** Role-based access for HODs, Directors, and the Director General for real-time monitoring.

## 4. SUCCESS CRITERIA
- **Efficiency:** Reduce reporting cycle time for CCSE staff by 80%.
- **Accuracy:** 100% alignment with the KSG FY 2025/2026 Performance Contract targets.
- **Scalability:** Architecture ready for School-wide adoption by Q1 2026.
